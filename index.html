    Who We Are

    According to SDG goal 8, we plan to tackle unemployment and make work decent for the youth. Everything is interconnected and we need to take a step to acheive this goal.

    Hirepesin is an innovative platform that is focused on connecting artisans and local craftsman from different parts of Africa to recruiters from various companies. We want to break the chain of unemployment and under expmployment amongst the youths and artisans.

    Hirepesin was founded by Jeff Dauda and Ahmed Bankole with the intention of providing an interactive platform for both job seekers and recruiters. Our goal is the make job opportunties available at your fingertips.
